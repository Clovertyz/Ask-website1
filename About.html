Padrino is a cozy Spanish restaurant dedicated to real, home-style cooking. We bring classic Spanish flavors to your table: small plates of tapas, saffron-scented paella, fresh seafood, and family recipes made with care.

Our dishes are prepared from high-quality, fresh ingredients and inspired by recipes passed down through generations. Whether you come for a quick tapa and a glass of wine or a long family meal, you'll find warm hospitality, rustic charm, and the taste of Spain.

Come relax in our welcoming space, enjoy lively conversation, and celebrate the simple joy of good food. We look forward to serving you.
